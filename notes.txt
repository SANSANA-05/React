props:
-----------
    ->props are used to transfer data from one to another Component
    ->props are immutable(we cannot change the data)
    ->props are in the form of key and value pair 
    ->props are present in both Calss and Function Based Component
        eg:<ChildComponent email={"roji@gmail.com"} skills = {["html","css","js"]} data = {{username: "Roji", password: "admin123@"}}/>
 

 States:
 ----------
    ->States are used to hold the data
    ->States are mutable in nature
    ->States are present only in Class Based Component
